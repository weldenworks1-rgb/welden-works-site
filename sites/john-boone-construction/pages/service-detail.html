The Service

Complete Storm
Restoration Process

After a major storm, stucco damage goes beyond what's visible. Water intrusion, structural movement, and impact damage can compromise large sections of your exterior — even when it looks fine on the surface.

We don't just patch what we see. We assess the full scope, identify underlying issues, and restore your exterior to pre-storm condition — or better.

1

Initial Assessment

We inspect your exterior for visible and hidden damage — including water infiltration, cracked lath, and compromised bonding layers.

2

Damage Mapping

We document all affected areas and provide a written scope of work with clear pricing before anything starts.

3

Demo & Prep

Damaged stucco removed to solid substrate. Moisture barrier and lath repaired or replaced as needed.

4

Multi-Coat Application

Scratch coat, brown coat, and finish coat applied in sequence. No shortcuts. Industry-standard cure times respected.

5

Texture Match & Blend

Final finish matched to your existing texture — knockdown, sand, smooth, or custom. Seamless from any angle.
