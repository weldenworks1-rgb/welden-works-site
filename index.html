Do I get to see the site before I pay? +
Yes — 100%. We build a full custom preview of your new website before you agree to anything. Zero cost, zero obligation. You only pay if you love it and want to go live.
How does the AI receptionist actually work? +
It's an AI-powered phone system that answers calls in your business name, asks qualifying questions, books appointments directly into your calendar, and sends follow-up SMS messages — 24/7, even when you're on the job.
How long does it take to go live? +
From the moment you approve your preview and we receive your domain information, your site is typically live within 48 hours. The AI receptionist is activated same day.
Do I need to provide content or photos? +
No. We research your business, write all the copy, and source professional imagery. If you have photos of your work you'd like to use, we'll incorporate them — but it's not required.
What if I want changes after the site is live? +
All plans include ongoing maintenance and updates as part of the monthly retainer. Need a new page, updated pricing, or a seasonal promotion? Just reach out and we handle it.
