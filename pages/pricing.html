Talk to Us →
Is there a long-term contract? +
No. All monthly plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. The only commitment is the one-time setup fee to get started.
What does the one-time fee cover? +
The setup fee covers everything needed to build and launch your service — design, development, AI training, integrations, and deployment. The monthly fee covers ongoing hosting, support, and updates.
Can I upgrade my plan later? +
Absolutely. You can add services or upgrade to a bundle at any time. We'll credit a portion of what you've already paid toward the upgrade.
Do I need the AI Receptionist to add SMS? +
Yes — SMS automation is an add-on to the AI Receptionist plan. The Pro Package includes both. If you want the full system, the Pro Package is the best value.
What happens if I cancel? +
You keep your website — you own it completely. We'll hand over all files and point your domain wherever you'd like. No hostage situations here.
Do you offer payment plans for setup fees? +
Yes, for Pro Package clients we can split the setup fee into two payments. Reach out and we'll work something out that makes sense for your business.
