Pricing Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

What's the difference between setup fee and monthly? +
The setup fee covers building and launching your deliverables — the website design, AI configuration, or SMS setup. The monthly retainer covers hosting, maintenance, support, and ongoing services like content updates and AI monitoring.
Are there long-term contracts? +
No. Monthly retainers can be cancelled anytime with 30 days notice. The setup fee is non-refundable since it covers the work done to build your deliverables — but you own everything we build.
Can I start with Starter and upgrade later? +
Absolutely. Many clients start with just a website and add the AI Receptionist or SMS services as they grow. We make upgrades seamless and credit any relevant previous setup fees toward the next tier.
Do I own my website and data? +
Yes — completely. Your website files, your domain, your lead data. If you ever leave, you take everything with you. No platform lock-in of any kind.
How do I get started? +
Fill out our contact form and we'll build a custom preview of your website for free — no commitment required. Once you love what you see, we'll discuss which plan is the right fit.
